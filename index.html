VAR

Video Assistant Referee

VAR stands for Video Assistant Referee. Its goal-line technology which was introduced in football to help determine if the ball crossed the line of the goal or not. Before the VAR was introduced you only had the referee who had to make the decision. They now use it for goal validity, red cards, penalty kicks and offsides. The first use of VAR was during a friendly match between France and Italy.

VR

Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality is virtual enviroment which uses cameras, sensors, joystick or anything else to immerse you in a different world. Pro-athletes can pratice their sport with the use of virtual reality and adjust to special situation which may happen on the field. Because they use virtual reality the pro-athletes cant be harmed by other players and can focus on the process and learn more skills to use during a competition on the field.

AI

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence will be used to identify the best teams in sport in the future. They can improving the team outcomes by making the best team by looking at skills, competencies and the spirit which will identify the beter athletes from around the world for the team. Artificial Intelligence can also help by using algorithms for important decisions during a match. The implementation of artificial intelligence will be game changing.
